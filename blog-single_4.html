Learning New Frameworks (Aur Thoda Pyaar)

July 30, 2025 • 5 mins read
Scroll

Kabhi socha hai coding aur pyaar kitne similar hote hain? Thoda funny lagta hai, but honestly, it's true.

When you discover a new framework—React, Flutter, Django—pehle toh sab naya-naya sa lagta hai. Exciting, shiny, full of possibilities. Bilkul waise hi jaise kisi naye insaan se milna. You spend late nights exploring it, learning, aur lagta hai ki bas ab yeh hi forever hai.

Phir aata hai reality check. Errors shuru ho jaate hain, documentation confusing lagti hai, aur chhoti-chhoti cheeze break hone lagti hain. That sweet love story suddenly feels messy. Coding ho ya love, honeymoon phase zyada time tikti nahi.

Aur yahan ek cliche hai pr sach hai: har mohabbat mukammal nhi hoti. Chahe tum kitni bhi desperately hold karo, kabhi-kabhi chhodna padta hai. Maine frameworks ke saath bhi yahi feel kiya, aur logon ke saath bhi. Both taught me the same thing—growth ka matlab hota hai samajhna ki kab aage badhna hai.

Letting go doesn't mean you failed. Matlab yeh hai ki wo chapter tumhari kahani ka ek part tha, puri kitab nahi. Kuch log aur kuch frameworks tumhe sirf ek certain time ke liye milte hain—taaki tumhe shape kar sake, kuch sikhaye, aur phir tumhe ready kar dein next cheez ke liye.

Aur jab sahi framework milta hai? Jaise React ya wo ek right person… toh sab click karta hai. Not because it's easy, but because tumhe grow karne mein madad karta hai. Tum feel karte ho ki tumhari efforts matter karte hain.

End of the day, coding aur love dono hi messy hain. Dono tumhe todte hain, fir banate hain. But each time you rebuild, tum thode smarter, thode stronger ho jaate ho. Aur shayad wahi hai asli journey ka maza.
